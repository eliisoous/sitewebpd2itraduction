Expanding Horizons: PD2i's DLC coating technology debuts in Indonesia

February 30, 2025 • By PD2i Team

PT Prima Nano Coating Pioneering Advanced Coating

We at PD2i are excited to share a significant achievement in our international expansion. PT Prima Nano Coating has become Indonesia's first provider of DLC coating services using our advanced technology.

This milestone represents not only our commitment to global growth but also our dedication to bringing cutting-edge coating solutions to emerging markets. Indonesia's rapidly growing manufacturing sector now has access to world-class DLC coating technology that will enhance the performance and durability of their industrial components.

Advanced Coatings Portfolio

PD2i is proud to announce that our Competence Center for tribological and functional coating have developed advanced coating portfolios in our Hybrid Coaters. Our Hybrid technology offers optimized solutions in relation with the applications:

  • For Plastics Molding - Enhanced surface properties for improved mold release and extended tool life
  • Cutting tools and Forming tools - Superior wear resistance and reduced friction for enhanced performance
  • Precision components - Tribological coatings for demanding applications requiring exceptional durability
